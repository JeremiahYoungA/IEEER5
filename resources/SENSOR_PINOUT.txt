COMPETITION HARDWARE PINOUT GUIDE
==================================

Microcontroller: ESP32-C6 Xiao Form Factor

RESERVED PINS (DO NOT USE):
- GPIO 5: I2C SDA (BME680)
- GPIO 6: I2C SCL (BME680)
- GPIO 21: LED (system indicator)

AVAILABLE ADC PINS for Analog Sensors:
- GPIO 0, 1, 2, 3, 4: Recommended for 3.3V analog inputs
- GPIO 8, 9: Alternative if GPIO 0-4 are exhausted

SENSORS AND WIRING:
==================

1. BME680 (Digital I2C Temperature/Humidity/Pressure/Gas)
   - Automatic: Connected via I2C (GPIO 5 = SDA, GPIO 6 = SCL)
   - I2C Address: 0x76 or 0x77 (use I2C scan to verify)
   - Library: Adafruit BME680
   - Wiring: 3.3V, GND, SDA->GPIO5, SCL->GPIO6

2. INA169 (Analog Current Sensor)
   - Output Pin: Analog Voltage proportional to current
   - Pin Assignment: Choose one ADC pin (GPIO 0-4)
   - Wiring: 3.3V, GND, OUT->ADC Pin
   - Calibration: Typical sensitivity 500mV per Amp
   - Formula: Current(A) = ADC_Voltage / 0.5

3. SPW2430 (Analog Microphone/Audio Input)
   - Output Pin: Analog audio level (center at 1.65V for 3.3V input)
   - Pin Assignment: Choose one ADC pin (GPIO 0-4)
   - Wiring: 3.3V, GND, OUT->ADC Pin
   - Note: Digital microphone - reads AC levels, not DC

4. CO Sensor V1.0 (Analog Carbon Monoxide Sensor)
   - Output Pin: Analog voltage (pinout unclear - verify on sensor)
   - Pin Assignment: Choose one ADC pin (GPIO 0-4)
   - Wiring: 3.3V, GND, A->ADC Pin
   - Note: Slow response time (~60s), may need warm-up period
